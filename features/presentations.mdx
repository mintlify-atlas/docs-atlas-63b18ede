## Overview

The presentation management system maintains a catalog of pharmaceutical product forms and packaging types. Presentations classify products by their physical form (tablets, capsules, syrup, etc.), which is essential for inventory organization, prescription accuracy, and customer service.

## Database Schema

The `presentacion` (presentation) table stores product form classifications:

| Field | Type | Constraints | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|-------------|
| `id` | int(11) | PRIMARY KEY, AUTO_INCREMENT | Unique presentation identifier |
| `nombre` | varchar(45) | NULL allowed | Presentation type name |

<Note>
Like the laboratory table, the presentation table has a simple structure with only an ID and name field. This lightweight design makes it easy to manage product form classifications.
</Note>

### Common Presentation Types

Pharmaceutical products come in various forms. Here are common examples to add to your system:

**Solid Forms:**
- **Tabletas** (Tablets): Compressed powder forms
- **Cápsulas** (Capsules): Gelatin shells containing medication
- **Comprimidos** (Pills): Similar to tablets, often coated
- **Grageas** (Dragées): Coated tablets
- **Polvos** (Powders): Loose powder form

**Liquid Forms:**
- **Jarabe** (Syrup): Sweet liquid medication
- **Suspensión** (Suspension): Particles suspended in liquid
- **Solución** (Solution): Dissolved medication in liquid
- **Gotas** (Drops): Liquid administered by drops
- **Ampollas** (Ampoules): Sealed vials for injection

**Topical Forms:**
- **Crema** (Cream): Emulsion for skin application
- **Pomada** (Ointment): Oil-based topical
- **Gel** (Gel): Semi-solid topical
- **Ungüento** (Salve): Healing ointment

**Other Forms:**
- **Inyectable** (Injectable): For injection
- **Óvulos** (Suppositories): For vaginal use
- **Parches** (Patches): Transdermal patches
- **Aerosol** (Aerosol/Spray): Pressurized spray

## Deleting Presentations

### Deletion Rules

Presentations can only be deleted if they have no associated products:

```sql
SELECT COUNT(*) as total 
FROM articulo 
WHERE presentacion_id = {id};
```

<Warning>
Presentations with associated products cannot be deleted. This protects data integrity and prevents orphaned product records. If the count is greater than 0, deletion is blocked with the error: "Esa presentación esta relacionado con al menos un artículo, no puede ser eliminado."
</Warning>

## Presentation Relationships

### Product Connection

Presentations are connected to products through a foreign key:

```sql
-- In articulo table
CONSTRAINT `fk_articulo_presentacion1` 
    FOREIGN KEY (`presentacion_id`) REFERENCES `presentacion` (`id`)
```

One presentation can have multiple products:

```
Presentation: Tabletas (Tablets)
├── Product: Aspirin 500mg Tablets
├── Product: Ibuprofen 400mg Tablets
└── Product: Paracetamol 500mg Tablets

Presentation: Jarabe (Syrup)
├── Product: Children's Cough Syrup 120ml
└── Product: Ambroxol Syrup 100ml
```

### Product Organization

Products are organized by presentation in several views:

**Product Creation Form:**
```sql
SELECT id as presentacion_id, nombre as presentacion_nombre 
FROM presentacion 
WHERE 1;
```

**Product Listing:**
```sql
SELECT 
    presentacion.nombre as presentacion,
    articulo.nombre,
    ...
FROM articulo, presentacion
WHERE presentacion_id = presentacion.id;
```

**Purchase and Sales Forms:**
Products are grouped by both laboratory and presentation for easy identification.

## Data Entry Tips

### Naming Conventions

Maintain consistency in presentation names:

**Correct:**
- ✅ "Tabletas"
- ✅ "Cápsulas"
- ✅ "Jarabe"

**Avoid:**
- ❌ "TABLETAS" (all caps)
- ❌ "tableta" (singular form - use plural)
- ❌ "Tabs" (abbreviations - use full words)

### Singular vs. Plural

Choose either singular or plural form and use consistently:

**Recommended: Plural**
- Tabletas, Cápsulas, Jarabes

**Alternative: Singular**
- Tableta, Cápsula, Jarabe

<Note>
The system doesn't enforce this, but consistency improves user experience and professional appearance.
</Note>

### Accent Marks

Use proper Spanish orthography:
- ✅ "Cápsulas" (with accent)
- ✅ "Solución" (with accent)
- ❌ "Capsulas" (missing accent)

## Reporting and Analytics

### Presentation Performance Reports

Use presentation data for business intelligence:

**Product Count by Presentation:**
```sql
SELECT 
    presentacion.nombre,
    COUNT(articulo.id) as total_products,
    SUM(articulo.stock) as total_stock
FROM presentacion
LEFT JOIN articulo ON presentacion.id = articulo.presentacion_id
GROUP BY presentacion.id
ORDER BY total_products DESC;
```

**Sales by Presentation:**
```sql
SELECT 
    presentacion.nombre,
    SUM(venta_detalle.cantidad) as units_sold,
    SUM(venta_detalle.cantidad * articulo.valor_venta) as total_sales
FROM presentacion
JOIN articulo ON presentacion.id = articulo.presentacion_id
JOIN venta_detalle ON articulo.id = venta_detalle.articulo_id
GROUP BY presentacion.id
ORDER BY total_sales DESC;
```

## Dosage vs. Presentation

### Understanding the Difference

- **Presentation**: Physical form (tablet, capsule, syrup)
- **Dosage**: Amount of active ingredient (500mg, 250mg)

**Example Product Classification:**
- Name: "Aspirin 500mg"
- Laboratory: "Bayer"
- Presentation: "Tabletas"

<Note>
Dosage information is typically included in the product name, while presentation is a separate classification field.
</Note>

## Combined Classification

Products are uniquely identified by the combination of:
1. **Name** (including dosage)
2. **Laboratory** (manufacturer)
3. **Presentation** (form)

This prevents duplicates:
- ✅ "Aspirin 500mg" + "Bayer" + "Tabletas"
- ✅ "Aspirin 500mg" + "Bayer" + "Cápsulas" (different presentation)
- ❌ "Aspirin 500mg" + "Bayer" + "Tabletas" (duplicate)
